
New features, beyond Escape Velocity Nova:

Auto-route (shortest path)

== Enterprise (multi-fleet) ==
- detatch ship(s) from main fleet, send on trading route
- ability to send order to detatched fleets to stay docked (multi-day docking costs extra)
- send ship from main fleet to a detatched fleet (with the ability to hire escorts to help it get there)


== Personnel (RPGish) ==
- Hire Captains and Commanders to Pilot Ships and Command Fleets (AI);
- Personnel gain their own experience and skills;
- Each Capital Ship owned by the enterprise must have a captain;
- Personnel can be chosen to participate in missions;
